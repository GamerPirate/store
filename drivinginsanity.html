ABOUT

Satisfying physics driving experience, levels difficulty range from easy to hardcore, from straight road to magic ramps, big jumps, and troll levels.

MODDED CARS
Every vehicle is unique with its own custom made version whether it's a tune to increase horsepower or a super dark glass film from Eclipse Inc. 

TROLL LEVELS
Many finish lines, but which one isn't fake? 
From magic disappearing ramps to many finish lines in one level. 

MAGIC RAMPS
Ramps the float in the sky and dissolve while you are standing on it 

CONTINUOUS UPDATES
Every update adds new features, new cars, and of course new levels. 

HIGH GRAPHICS
Realistic graphics, high quality models, talented vehicle paint, and realistic colors. 

EARLY ACCESS
Driving Insanity is currently in early access which means some of the game features are not there yet and a limited number of levels and cars is in the game, each update adds new features, new cars, and new levels, if you found any bug or have any suggestion consider contacting Gamer Pirate.

SPECIFICATIONS

Minimum Specifications
Processor: AMD Athlon X2 or Intel Core 2 Duo
GPU: AMD HD4200 or Intel G45
RAM: 1.5GB
DirectX: 10
OS: Windows 7 32 bit
HDD Space: 150MB



Recommended Specifications
Processor: AMD Phenom X4 or Intel Core i3 2125
GPU: Nvidia GT1030 or AMD Radeon HD 8950
RAM: 4GB 
DirectX: 10.1
OS: Windows 10 64 bit 
HDD Space: 300MB
