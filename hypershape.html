Once you are ready throw an arrow!

Presenting HYPERSHAPE, based on an old arcade game I made back in 2016. Your goal is to throw all the arrows you have to the core, if you miss by throwing an arrow onto an existing arrow you fail, easy as that.

Features:
• 10,000 Levels
• Dark themed
• Clean look
• High quality sounds
• Offline gameplay
