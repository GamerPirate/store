ABOUT

Just escape the obstacles and reach the highest score you can achieve.

ENDLESS MODE
Go as long as you can. Compete with real players and try to get to the number 1 spot in the leaderboard 

CUSTOMIZATION 
Choose your player from a variety of player shapes, 
Escape in style. 

DIFFICULTY 
Just Escape starts with low rotation speeds and as you progress obstacles speed up. 

SCORE 
Every successful escape adds a score point to your score, 
Every 5 obstacles you get a coin as a reward, this coin is multiplied with the coin number so the more you progress the bigger the reward, 
Same thing for the rare diamond but you get it every 50 obstacles, that's why it's rare.
