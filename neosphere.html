ABOUT

Welcome to the neoniverse, where everything is neon. Your goal is to survive. Many obstacles and flying cubes will try to make you fail, but do you have what it takes to beat them all?

It's known that for every action there's a reaction. So why won't you get rewarded after surviving! For every obstacle you pass you receive coins and score points that will later make you able to purchase and customize your neosphere.
